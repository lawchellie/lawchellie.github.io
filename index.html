About Us

Welcome to Abuja, the most beautify city in Africa. Located in the very centre of Nigeria and in the heart of Africa, Abuja blends nature with endowned breathe taking sites. By displaying our tradition of warmth and impeccable service delivery, the Marina Boylos Hotel is poised to meet your every desire. Conveniently located in the heart of the Federal capital, the Marina Boyblos Hotel is only 30 minutes' drive away from Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport and is nestled within the many tourist sights and sounds of Abuja. Take in the magnificent views of the city from one of our Suites and feel right at home.
